# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, gender, orientation, disability, ethnicity, nationality, or experience level.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Respectful communication  
- Constructive feedback  
- Collaboration and support  
- Focus on helping the community grow

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination  
- Personal attacks  
- Publishing private information  
- Any conduct that may cause harm to individuals or the community

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for ensuring safe participation. They may remove comments, code changes, or contributors that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Scope
This Code applies within all project spaces: repository, issues, discussions, and other channels.
