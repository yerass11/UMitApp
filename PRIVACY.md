UMitApp handles user data responsibly and transparently.  
This document describes how the app processes and protects user information.

## Data We Collect
UMitApp may collect the following:
- Email and authentication data (via Firebase)
- Appointment and order history
- Chat messages with medical specialists
- Optional payment information (via Stripe)

All data collection is used solely to provide app functionality.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT store plaintext passwords  
- We do NOT sell or share data with third parties  
- We do NOT collect unnecessary personal information  

## How We Keep Data Secure
- Authentication handled by Firebase  
- Payment information processed securely by Stripe  
- Backend protected by authentication tokens  
- All network traffic uses HTTPS-only

## User Rights
Users may:
- Request deletion of their account  
- Request access to their stored data  
- Disable notifications
